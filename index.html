Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about SL FloodWatch and flood safety in Sierra Leone.

How often is the water level data updated?
Data is transmitted from our IoT sensors every 15 minutes to ensure real-time accuracy across all monitored zones.
How do I receive emergency alerts?
Currently, alerts are broadcast on this dashboard. We are actively working on an SMS notification system for users in high-risk zones.
Who validates the community reports?
Our moderation team and local community leaders verify reports before they are broadcast as official alerts.

About SL FloodWatch

Built for Sierra Leone

A Digital Public Good (DPG) initiative combining community reports, IoT sensor data, and government partnerships to protect lives from flooding.

📡

Real-time Sensors

River level and rainfall sensors deployed across 14 districts feeding live data every 15 minutes.

📲

Community Reports

Citizens submit on-the-ground reports via this website or SMS, verified and broadcast within minutes.

🏛️

Government Partnership

Integrated with Sierra Leone's National Disaster Management Agency (NDMA) for coordinated response.

🔓

Open Source (DPG)

Fully open-source under Digital Public Goods principles. Free to use, adapt, and improve by anyone.
